# Clean Up Presentation

## Description
Do a full formatting and visual cleanup pass on the presentation so it looks polished and consistent from start to finish. The goal is a deck that reads as one cohesive, on-brand document — consistent typography, spacing, and alignment throughout, with clean, well-composed visuals and layouts on every slide. Success means the deck is presentation-ready with no visual rough edges.

## Context
- Presentation: https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/13h9uARrk3zRMxb2TwP_jvNRLkcOT8HsYfHwD2XVPYp8/edit?slide=id.p33#slide=id.p33
- Scope covers the **whole deck** (all slides), not just the linked section.
- Focus is on **formatting & consistency** and **visuals & layout** — not rewriting content.
- Due: **2026-07-23 (tomorrow) by 2:00 PM**.

## Subtasks
- [ ] Audit the full deck and note formatting/visual inconsistencies (fonts, sizes, colors, spacing, misaligned elements)
- [ ] Standardize typography — consistent fonts, sizes, weights, and text colors across all slides
- [ ] Fix alignment, spacing, and margins so elements line up consistently slide to slide
- [ ] Apply consistent brand colors and styling throughout
- [ ] Clean up visuals — charts, images, diagrams, and slide layouts (crop/replace low-quality assets, tidy compositions)
- [ ] Final review pass across the full deck to confirm consistency end to end

## Acceptance Criteria
- Typography (font family, sizes, weights, colors) is consistent across every slide.
- All elements are aligned and spacing/margins are uniform slide to slide.
- Colors and styling follow a single consistent brand look.
- Charts, images, and diagrams are clean, sharp, and well-composed.
- The full deck has been reviewed and is presentation-ready by the due date.

## Out of Scope
- Rewriting or restructuring the content/copy.
- Adding new slides or new content.
- Removing or reordering slides.
- Fact-checking or updating data and numbers.
